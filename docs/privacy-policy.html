Privacy Policy

Mafia: Social Deduction Game

Last updated: April 2026

🔒 Short version: We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. No account is required to play.

📋 Information We Collect

Mafia: Social Deduction Game does not require account creation or any form of registration. The only information used during a session is:

  • A display name you choose when joining a game (e.g. "Alex" or an emoji)
  • An avatar emoji you select for your in-game avatar

These are temporary, session-only identifiers. They are never stored on our servers after your session ends and are never linked to any real-world identity.

🚫 What We Do Not Collect

  • No name, email address, or phone number
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No location data
  • No payment or financial information
  • No contacts, photos, or files from your device
  • No usage analytics or crash reports sent to third parties

💬 In-Game Chat

Chat messages sent during a game are relayed in real time to other players in the same room. Messages are held in memory only for the duration of the game session and are permanently deleted when the session ends. We do not log, store, or review chat content.

🤖 AI Bots

When playing against AI bots, game state information (player names chosen by you, game actions) may be sent to Groq's API to generate bot responses. No personally identifiable information is included in these requests. Please refer to Groq's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle API data.

🔐 Data Security

All communication between the app and our server is encrypted in transit using TLS/HTTPS. Since we do not store personal data, there is no personal data at risk of a breach.

👶 Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the app, please contact us and we will address it promptly.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
