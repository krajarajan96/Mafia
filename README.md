# Can you spot the Mafia before it's too late?

Mafia is the ultimate social deduction game — play online with friends or jump into a solo match against AI bots that actually think, chat, and bluff like real players.

---

## 🌙 How it works?

Each round plays out in three phases:

1. **🌙 Night** — Secret actions happen in the dark. Mafia picks a target. The Detective investigates. The Doctor protects. The Vigilante takes aim. No one knows what anyone else is doing.
2. **☀️ Discussion** — The town wakes up and debates. Who's acting suspicious? Who's too quiet? Who's deflecting? Use the group chat to argue your case.
3. **🗳️ Voting** — The town votes to eliminate a suspect. Get it right and you're one step closer to victory. Get it wrong and the Mafia grows stronger and stronger.

---

## 🎭 6 Unique Roles

| Role | Description |
| --- | --- |
| 🔪 Mafia | Eliminate Town players each night without getting caught |
| 🕵️ Detective | Investigate one player per night to reveal their true side |
| 💉 Doctor | Protect a player from the Mafia's kill each night |
| 🤠 Vigilante | Shoot a suspect, but beware — shoot an innocent and you both die |
| 💃 Escort | Block a player's night action to disrupt their plans |
| 🏛️ Minister | Hold a secret one-time veto to cancel any elimination |
